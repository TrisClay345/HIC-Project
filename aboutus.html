WHO ARE WE?

 Born in 863 in Sascotto, Italy, Lotsa Sauce was raised by his father Alltha, and his mother Aniidmo. After an encounter with the Pastafia, Lotsa had to find a cover operation to get out of Italy, and fast food spaghetti was right up his alley. Now, for over 1000 years, he has run his small business for everyone in Nebraska. In 1963, Lotsa expanded to include three more restaurants inside of New York City run by his daughter, Globbagool.
